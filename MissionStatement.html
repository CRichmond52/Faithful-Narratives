Mission Statement

Hard words, old book. That's the lifeblood of Faithful Narratives. David Wells wrestles the Bible, digs deep for what matters. No sugarcoating, just the core. Timeless truths, hidden beneath the surface. Wells brings them to light, clean and clear. No blind faith here. We offer a straight shot through scripture, a chance to understand its weight. Not a feel-good journey, but a chance to grapple with the text, see if it speaks to you. Like a hard climb, rewards the effort. Join us, if you're ready for the fight.
