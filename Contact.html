Contact

To connect with David Wells, the author of "Faithful Narratives," you can easily reach out via email at davidwells@eml.cc. Crafting a message to David is a great opportunity to share your thoughts and discuss any questions or insights it may have sparked.  

Whether you have inquiries about the themes or simply want to share your thoughts, David is likely eager to engage with readers who are passionate about his writing. Remember to be respectful of his time and keep your message concise and clear. Who knows? Your email might just lead to a meaningful exchange with the author himself.
