My Story

David Wells, the pen name of a seasoned writer, draws from over five decades of faith experience to inspire and enlighten readers. With a wealth of life’s lessons accumulated through years of dedication, David’s writings reflect a deep-rooted understanding of spirituality and human experience.​

Married for over fifty years, David has cultivated a rich tapestry of familial bonds, serving as a testament to enduring love and commitment. His journey as a husband and father to two daughters and a grandfather to three granddaughters infuses his work with warmth, empathy, and insight into the dynamics of relationships and family life.​

In his professional career, David has traversed diverse paths, bringing a breadth of experience to his writing. From his role as a retired purchasing agent, where he honed his negotiation skills and attention to detail, to his tenure as a retired electric engineering aid, where he demonstrated technical proficiency and problem-solving prowess, David’s background informs his nuanced perspectives on life’s challenges and triumphs.

As an author, David’s passion for storytelling and exploration shines through in his work. A dedicated researcher and lover of the Word, he delves into themes of faith, resilience, and personal growth, offering readers profound insights and encouragement on their own spiritual journeys.

Beyond writing, David channels his creative energy into photography, capturing moments of beauty and inspiration. His keen eye for detail and appreciation for the world around him infuse his photography with depth and meaning, complementing his literary endeavors.

David’s pursuit of knowledge and understanding led him to obtain a Bachelor of Theology degree (B.Th.), enriching his writing with theological depth and insight. Grounded in his faith and fueled by a relentless curiosity, David continues to inspire readers with his words, inviting them to explore the depths of their own spirituality and the wonders of the human experience.
