My Story

Five decades. Faith. Words. That's David Wells. Not a preacher, a writer. Life lessons etched deep. Family man, fifty years strong. Love, commitment - the real deal. Negotiated deals, fixed wires - all part of the story. Challenges, triumphs - he's seen them all.

Words are his tools. Dives deep, finds the core. Faith, resilience, growing as a person - that's what matters. No sugarcoating, just the truth. He pushes you to think, see if it resonates.

More than words. Captures beauty with a camera. Details matter, the world matters. Photography fuels his writing, both tell a story.

Lifelong learner. Earned a degree, theology his game. Faith is the foundation, curiosity the engine. Words inspire, stories invite. Explore yourself, the human journey - that's the message.
